Tsukimi

A Japanese festival celebrating the autumn moon, often with offerings of rice dumplings.

Mid autumn - China

A Chinese festival honoring the full moon with mooncakes, lanterns, and family gatherings.

Mid autumn - Vietnam

A Vietnamese festival featuring lion dances, lantern parades, and mooncakes.

Thadingyut Festival

A Buddhist festival in Myanmar marking the end of the Buddhist Lent, celebrated with lanterns and moonlit prayers.

Bon Om Touk

The Cambodian Water Festival, held during the full moon, featuring boat races and moonlight festivities.

Loy Krathong

A Thai festival where people float lanterns on rivers to show gratitude and wash away misfortunes.

Duruthu Poya

A full moon day in Sri Lanka marking Buddha's first visit to the island.

Chotrul Duchen

A Tibetan Buddhist festival celebrating the miracles of Buddha, held on the full moon.

Moonlight Festival Sofia

Held in Sofia, Bulgaria, this festival transforms buildings and architecture into stunning light artworks.

Janai Purnima

A Hindu festival in Nepal where people bathe in holy rivers and celebrate under the full moon.

Hunter's Moon

A Native American tradition marking the full moon after the Harvest Moon, used for hunting preparation.

Sigi Festival

A Dogon tribal festival in Mali, celebrated once every 60 years, linked to the Sirius star and moon cycles.
